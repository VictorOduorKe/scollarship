Athlete Scholarship Requirements

To apply for an athlete scholarship, applicants typically need to meet the following criteria:

  • 1. Academic Requirements

    Minimum GPA (e.g., 2.5 - 3.0 on a 4.0 scale)

    High school diploma or equivalent

    Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT) if required

    Transcripts from previous educational institutions

  • 2. Athletic Performance

    Proof of competitive sports experience (e.g., participation in high school, club, or national-level teams)

    Performance statistics and achievements (e.g., personal bests, rankings, records)

    Video highlights or game footage showcasing skills

    Recommendation letters from coaches or sports officials

  • 3. Physical and Medical Requirements

    Physical fitness assessment results

    Proof of no serious injuries or medical conditions affecting performance

    Medical examination and clearance from a certified doctor

  • 4. Character and Conduct

    Letter of recommendation from a coach, teacher, or mentor

    Good disciplinary record (no major violations or suspensions)

    Demonstrated leadership, teamwork, and sportsmanship

  • 5. Application Documents

    Completed scholarship application form

    Personal statement or essay explaining goals and motivatio

    Resume/CV highlighting academic and athletic achievements

    Proof of nationality or residency (if required)

  • 6. Additional Requirements (Varies by Institution)

    Participation in tryouts or showcases

    Agreement to maintain academic and athletic performance standards

    Compliance with NCAA, NAIA, or other governing body regulations (if applicable)
